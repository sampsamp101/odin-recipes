Oven Barbecued Beef Brisket

Ingredients

1. The Beef

  • 3–4 lbs beef chuck roast or beef short ribs

3. Dry Rub

  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp cayenne pepper
  • ½ tsp cumin

BBQ Sauce

  • 1½ cups ketchup
  • ¼ cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 300°F (150°C)
  2. Mix all dry rub ingredients together
  3. Pat beef dry and coat all sides generously with the rub
  4. Let rest at room temperature for 30 minutes
  5. Heat an oven-safe pan over high heat with a little oil
  6. Sear the beef 3–4 minutes per side until browned
  7. Mix all BBQ sauce ingredients in a bowl
  8. Pour half the sauce over the beef in the pan
  9. Cover tightly with foil or a lid
  10. Bake for 3–3.5 hours until fork tender
  11. Uncover, pour remaining sauce over the top
  12. Increase oven to 400°F and bake uncovered 20–25 minutes to caramelize
  13. Let rest 10 minutes before slicing or pulling
  14. Serve with extra sauce on the side
