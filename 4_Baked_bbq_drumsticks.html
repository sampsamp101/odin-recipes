Baked BBQ Chicken Drumsticks

Ingredients

  • 8 chicken drumsticks
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoons salt
  • 0.5 teaspoons black pepper
  • 1 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoons smoked paprika
  • 0.5 teaspoons onion powder
  • 1.5 cups BBQ sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a large baking sheet with foil or parchment paper and set a wire rack on top, if you have one.
  2. Season the drumsticks: Pat 8 chicken drumsticks dry with paper towels. In a large bowl, toss them with 2 tablespoons olive oil, 1 teaspoons salt, 0.5 teaspoons black pepper, 1 teaspoons garlic powder, 1 teaspoons smoked paprika, and 0.5 teaspoons onion powder until evenly coated.
  3. Arrange on baking sheet: Place the seasoned drumsticks in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet, leaving space between each piece so they crisp up nicely.
  4. First bake: Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes
  5. Brush with BBQ sauce: Remove the drumsticks from the oven and brush generously with about half of the 1.5 cups BBQ sauce, turning to coat all sides.
  6. Second bake: Return to the oven and bake for another 17m 30s until the internal temperature reaches 175°F (80°C) and the skin is caramelized.
  7. Final glaze and rest: Brush with the remaining 1.5 cups BBQ sauce, then let the drumsticks rest for 5 minutes before serving.
