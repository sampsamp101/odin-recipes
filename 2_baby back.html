Oven Baked Baby Back Ribs Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 racks baby back ribs (~4 lbs)
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 275°F (135°C)
  2. Remove the membrane from the back of the ribs
  3. Mix all dry spices together to make the rub
  4. Coat ribs generously on both sides with the rub
  5. Wrap each rack tightly in aluminum foil
  6. Place on a baking sheet and bake for 2.5–3 hours
  7. Unwrap ribs and brush with BBQ sauce
  8. Increase oven to 400°F and bake uncovered for 15–20 minutes
  9. Let rest 5 minutes before slicing and serving
